About Me

Welcome!

I'm a retired sommelier with over 30 years of experience exploring the world of wine—from the rolling vineyards of Bordeaux to the hidden gems of Napa Valley. Throughout my career, I’ve had the privilege of curating wine lists for top restaurants, guiding countless tastings, and discovering exceptional bottles that tell their own unique stories.

This website is my new chapter—a place where I share the stories, insights, and adventures I’ve collected over the years. Whether you’re a seasoned enthusiast or just beginning your journey into wine, I invite you to join me as we explore the rich world of reds, whites, and rosés. Here, you’ll find personal anecdotes, tasting notes, travel tales, and thoughtful recommendations that I hope will inspire your own wine journey.

Pour a glass, stay awhile, and let’s enjoy the conversation.
